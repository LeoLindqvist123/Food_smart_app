# FoodSmart
An AI-powered iPhone app that turns your camera into a personal nutritionist. Snap a photo of your meal or fridge, and FoodSmart instantly estimates calories, breaks down macros, and suggests recipes based on what you have.

## The Problem
Tracking what you eat is tedious. Most calorie apps require you to manually search for every ingredient, guess portion sizes, and log each meal by hand. People give up within a week.

## The Solution
FoodSmart removes the friction. Point your camera at your plate or open fridge, and AI vision does the rest — identifying foods, estimating portions, calculating nutrition, and even suggesting what to cook with the ingredients you already have.

## Why FoodSmart?
There are calorie trackers out there. There are recipe apps. But none of them connect the two in a seamless way.

Most food scanning apps stop at "that's an apple, 95 calories." FoodSmart goes further — when you scan your fridge, it doesn't just list what's inside. It tells you what you can actually cook with it. Real recipes, tailored to your ingredients, with full nutritional breakdowns.

The focus is not just counting calories. It's helping you eat better with what you already have.
 
## How It Works
1. Snap — Take a photo of your food or fridge contents
2. Scan — AI vision identifies every item and estimates quantities
3. Results — Get instant calorie count, macro breakdown (protein, carbs, fat), and confidence scores
4. Recipes — Receive recipe suggestions based on detected ingredients
